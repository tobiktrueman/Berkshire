About us

We specialise in the producing of the timber stairs and the wooden floors of the finest quality. In our offer we have a large variety of components and accessories. Our stairs, balustrades, rails, floors are manufactured with the highest precision and are always being designed to be easy to use.

We are located in Reading, Berkshire, UK We suply and fit stairs in England and EU

Feel free to familiarize with our offer. We provide free of charge technical consultation which will enable you to choose an appropriate solution. Before you make a decision we are able to prepare a visual presentation of your project and thanks to our modern technology, production and assembly processes are fast and highly precise.

The philosophy of our production is based on the experienced staff and modern technology. Our experience lets us to undertake event most ambitious challenges.
